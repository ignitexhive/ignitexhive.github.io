Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-11

Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how IgniteX Hive ("IgniteX Hive", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you access our website, purchase tickets, or participate in distributions. We process personal data in line with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

Data We Collect

  • Identifiers such as name, email, mobile number, wallet address, and Tag ID.
  • Transaction data including ticket purchases, entries, wins, and payouts.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device information, browser details, and cookies.
  • Verification data requested for AML/CFT screening when required.

Purpose of Processing

  • Provide authentication, account access, and payout processing.
  • Deliver customer support, service updates, and operational notices.
  • Monitor and mitigate fraud, abuse, or security threats.
  • Analyse platform performance and, with consent, send marketing updates.

Legal Basis

We rely on consent, contractual necessity, legal obligations (including AMLA 2001), and legitimate interests such as security, fraud prevention, and platform improvement.

Sharing & Transfers

  • Service providers that support hosting, messaging, analytics, or payments.
  • Financial institutions and compliance partners conducting AML/CFT checks.
  • Regulators or law enforcement agencies when required by law.

Retention

We retain personal data while your account remains active and as needed for legal, tax, audit, or regulatory obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent subject to PDPA exceptions. Contact ignitexhive@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Security

We apply encryption in transit, access controls, logging, and breach notification protocols to safeguard personal data.

Cross-Border Processing

Data may be stored or processed outside Malaysia with contractual safeguards and technical controls to protect privacy.

Anti-Corruption & Audit Trail

Records are maintained to support compliance with the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission Act and related audits.

Environmental Responsibility

IgniteX Hive prioritises efficient digital operations and selects vendors that adopt responsible environmental practices.

Updates

We may update this Policy to reflect regulatory or operational changes. A new effective date will appear above when updates occur.
