How to Send Ripple (XRP) in Luno

11-step visual guide • QR-based address entry • Compliance • Proof capture
1Step 1 – Open Send Menu
On the Luno home screen, tap the 3-dot menu at the top-right corner. From the menu options, select Send.
2Step 2 – Choose Crypto & Confirm Network
From the list of coins, select Ripple (XRP). Luno only supports the XRP Ledger network. Warning: Sending on other networks (ERC20, BEP20) will permanently lose funds.
3Step 3 – Network Warning
Luno shows: “Avoid losing crypto - make sure the destination wallet supports XRP Ledger.” Read carefully, then tap Continue with Send.
4Step 4 – Enter Wallet Address
Do not type addresses manually. Instead, scan the QR Code from the Master Copy document:
  • Successors → scan their QR Code
  • Predecessors → scan their QR Code
  • IgniteX Hive (Company) → company QR Code
5Step 5 – Enter Amount
Enter the transfer value in MYR only (standard: RM10, RM30, RM50). Add a note/reference if needed.
6Step 6 – DRB Status
Answer compliance questions:
  • Do you have Domestic Ringgit Borrowing (DRB)?
  • Is the transfer Local or Overseas?
7Step 7 – Confirm Address Owner
Double-check the address belongs to the correct type. Always choose “This is someone else’s address.” for all successors, predecessors, and IgniteX Hive.
8Step 8 – Recipient Type
Select recipient type:
  • Successors & Predecessors → A person
  • IgniteX Hive (Company) → A business/organisation
9Step 9 – Recipient Details
Enter the recipient’s Name and Nationality. For IgniteX Hive, enter company details.
10Step 10 – Exchange Source
Choose where the recipient’s address comes from:
  • Successors & Predecessors → Another exchange → Luno
  • IgniteX Hive (Company) → Private wallet → Ledger
. .
11Step 11 – Final Confirmation & Proof

Review your transaction carefully:

  • ✔️ Wallet address from QR Code
  • ✔️ Destination Tag (if required)
  • ✔️ Amount in MYR
  • ✔️ Recipient type (Person/Business)
  • ✔️ Source (Luno or Ledger)
  • ✔️ Enter 2FA code and confirm

📸 After completion

Take a full screenshot of the successful transaction screen using the same phone used for the transfer.

The screenshot must clearly show:

  • 🔹 Title: “Sent Ripple to …”
  • 🔹 XRP amount sent
  • 🔹 Timestamp (date & time)
  • 🔹 XRP Tag (if applicable)
  • 🔹 Transaction ID

❌ The screenshot must remain untampered and unedited.

⏳ Wait up to 5 minutes for the transaction to complete.
