Step 1

Download the Luno App & Sign Up

  1. Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Search for Luno.
  3. Tap Install and wait until the download finishes.
  4. Open the app and tap Get Started.
  5. Enter your email and create a password.
  6. Enter referral link if not pre-filled: https://www.luno.com/invite/4QHXJ9
  7. Confirm your email through the verification email.
Step 2

Verify Your Identity

  1. Tap Profile + Verify identity.
  2. Provide your full legal name and date of birth.
  3. Upload a clear photo of your NRIC / MyKad (front & back).
  4. Take a selfie for verification.
  5. Wait for confirmation (usually a few hours).
  6. Important: Without verification, deposits and withdrawals are disabled.
Step 3

Enable Send Crypto Feature

  1. From the Home screen, tap Wallets.
  2. Choose XRP (Ripple).
  3. Tap Send.
  4. Follow prompts to unlock sending.
  5. Some users may be asked to confirm additional identity checks.
  6. Note: Once enabled, you cannot reverse a send transaction.
Step 4

Set Up Ripple (XRP) Wallet

  1. Go to Wallets + Add Wallet + Ripple (XRP).
  2. Luno will generate a unique wallet address.
  3. XRP uses both a Wallet Address and a Tag ID.
  4. Never send XRP without the correct Tag ID. Missing the tag may result in permanent loss of funds.
Step 5

Deposit Malaysian Ringgit (MYR)

  1. Go to Wallets + MYR Wallet + Deposit.
  2. Choose Bank Transfer (FPX).
  3. Enter amount: RM155.00.
  4. Follow on-screen bank login steps and confirm.
  5. Deposit may take a few minutes to appear.
Step 6

Buy XRP (Ripple)

  1. Go to Buy/Sell.
  2. Select Buy.
  3. Choose XRP (Ripple).
  4. Select One-off Purchase.
  5. Enter amount RM155.
  6. Confirm and finalize the purchase. (Rates vary - check before confirming.)
Step 7

Send XRP to IgniteX Hive Distribution Wallets

  1. Send XRP in the following distribution:
  2. Company Wallet (Gen 00): RM50
  3. Predecessors (Gen 01-07): RM10 each
  4. Current Successor (Gen 08): RM30
  5. Total: RM155   |   Transaction Fee: ~RM5 (XRP equivalent)
  6. Always confirm the Wallet Address and Tag ID before sending.
Step 8

Submit Proof of Transaction

  1. Take screenshots of each successful transaction.
  2. Visit the Google Form (link will be provided).
  3. Enter: Your Wallet Address and Your Tag ID.
  4. Upload your screenshots and submit the form.
  5. Mandatory: Proof submission is required for confirmation.
Tip: Keep your Luno app updated. Enable biometric login (Face/Touch ID) and 2FA for added security.
