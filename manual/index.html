Updated 2025-09-04
1 Step 1 – Open Send Menu
On the Luno home screen, tap the 3-dot menu at the top-right corner, then select Send.
2 Step 2 – Choose Crypto & Confirm Network
From the list of coins, select Ripple (XRP). Luno supports the XRP Ledger network for XRP.
3 Step 3 – Network Warning
Read the warning carefully (avoid losing crypto). Tap Continue with Send to proceed on XRP Ledger.
4 Step 4 – Enter Wallet Address
Scan the QR Code from the Master Copy document:
  • Successors → scan their QR Code
  • Predecessors → scan their QR Code
  • IgniteX Hive (Company) → company QR Code
5 Step 5 – Enter Amount
Enter the transfer value in MYR only (RM10, RM30, RM50). Add a note if needed.
6 Step 6 – DRB Status
Answer compliance questions (DRB) and whether the transfer is Local or Overseas.
7 Step 7 – Confirm Address Owner
Select “This is someone else’s address”.
8 Step 8 – Recipient Type
Recipient type:
  • Successors/Predecessors → A person
  • IgniteX Hive → A business/organisation
9 Step 9 – Recipient Details
Enter recipient’s Name and Nationality. For IgniteX Hive, enter company details.
10 Step 10 – Exchange Source
Choose source of address:
  • Successors/Predecessors → Another exchange → Luno
  • IgniteX Hive → Private wallet → Ledger
. .
11 Step 11 – Final Confirmation & Proof

Review carefully: address, tag, MYR amount, recipient type, and source, then enter 2FA and confirm.

After completion: Take a full screenshot of the successful transaction on the same phone used to transfer. Ensure it shows Title, XRP amount, Timestamp, XRP Tag, and Transaction ID. Do not edit or apply effects. Wait up to 5 minutes, then check your XRP Wallet portfolio to confirm under Send Ripple. Attach the screenshot to the IgniteX Hive Larvae Form as proof.
